簡婉軒 Woan-Shiuan Chien (Winnie)

Assistant Professor at NYCU

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering of National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), starting from Feb. 2026. I am the director of Social Lab (人本訊號運算實驗室).

I received my Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from National Tsing Hua University (NTHU) in 2025, under the supervision of Prof. Chi-Chun (Jeremy) Lee in the BIIC Lab.

📢 [Dear prospective students]: I am actively recruiting motivated undergraduate / Master / Ph.D. students to join our lab!
If you are eager to learn Multimodal Signal Processing / Affective Computing / Human-Centered Analytics / Data Science / Trustworthy AI, or if you are interested in real-world applications such as healthcare, LLM reasoning, and speech emotion recognition systems, I warmly welcome you to reach out. Please email me directly with your CV and transcripts.

My research interests revolve around human-centered analytics, affective computing, machine learning, and multimodal signal processing across speech, physiology, and gesture. Recently, I have broadened my research scope toward trustworthy AI, focusing on privacy, fairness, and bias within emotion recognition technologies. I also develop machine learning and deep learning models for emotion recognition, dyadic interaction, and healthcare applications.

Emotion Recognition

Investigating perceptual fairness by identifying and mitigating group and individual biases in emotion recognition systems, with the goal of improving reliability and credibility in affective computing applications.

Trustworthy AI

Exploring transparent, fair, and responsible machine learning mechanisms that can better serve diverse people across real-world contexts and sensitive application domains.
