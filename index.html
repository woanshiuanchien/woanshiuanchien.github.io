About Me

Hi I'm Woan-Shiuan (Winnie) Chien. A third-year Ph.D. student in Behavioral Informatics & Interaction Lab (BIIC Lab) at National Tsing Hua University, interesting in Group Interaction Behavior, Affective Multimedia, and Analysis area, especially for behavioral signal processing.

My researches focus on the human-centered behavioral studies using physiology. We believe that there are latent patterns exist in natural human expression and dedicate to discover how human beings express their feelings/emotions and behaviors.
